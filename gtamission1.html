#Franklin and Lamar Mission

In the "Franklin and Lamar" mission in GTA V, after the prologue and Michael's credit scene, Franklin and Lamar are tasked with repossessing two cars for Simeon Yetarian. The mission involves a high-speed chase through the streets of Los Santos as the characters pursue the debtors and attempt to retrieve the vehicles. The mission serves as an introduction to the driving mechanics of the game and the open-world environment. Players must navigate through traffic, evade the police, and work together with Lamar to successfully complete the repo job and deliver the cars to Simeon's dealership. This mission sets the tone for the game's action-packed sequences and showcases the dynamic and immersive gameplay of Grand Theft Auto V.
